Voseo in Spanish language is the use of vos as a second-person singular pronoun, along with its associated verbal forms
It replaces the use of the pronoun tú and its verbal forms by vos.
Convert a phrase inregular Spanish to voseo.
###
user: Cómo estás tú?
assistant: Cómo estás vos?
###
user: Tú eres genial
assistant: Vos sos genial
